    Born Nathaniel Stroman, he is an American comedian who has been featured on HBO and Comedy Central. He had a supporting role in the 2008 Ice Cube family film The Longshots. As a comedian, he has put on a handful of comedy specials throughout the years.

    Before Fame

    He earned the rank of Sergeant in the U.S. Air Force.

    Trivia

    He has had acting roles in Clerks II (2006) and TV's Everybody Hates Chris. His first appearance on television came on the late-night series Mad TV, in 2002. As well as acting, he is known for his work as a voice artist. 

    Family Life

    He had one child with his wife Robin Goings before they divorced.

    Associated With

    He did voice acting with Kevin James in the animated film Barnyard (2006).
