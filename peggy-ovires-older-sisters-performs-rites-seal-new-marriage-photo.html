    Peggy Ovires Older Sisters Performs Rites, Seal New Marriage (Photo)

    31 Mar 2024, 00:00

    A photo capturing the moment the older sisters of newly wedded Peggy Ovire performed prayer rites thereby sealing her marriage with her husband, Frederick Leonard in Christ has surfaced on social media.

    It is no longer news that the lovebirds tied the knot on Saturday, 19th November in Warri, Delta State, amidst bliss and well-wishers.

    Sharing one of the special moments from the occasion, Peggy revealed how her sisters who have been married for over 30 years sealed her marriage with her colleague, Fredrick Leonard.

    She added that she came from a family who always put God first, and had called to put her marriage under God’s guidance and protection.

    Sharing the photo, she captioned it: “This right here was the Highlight of the Night for me, I was Raised in a family who puts God First in Everything & Grew up seeing my 2 big sisters Love God Fervently. The eldest ,(left ) have been Married for 30yrs & my other sister Gifty (right )Married for 21yrs & still Counting. I don’t know how they do it but having them Cover us under Gods guidance,protection and Me following in their footsteps is a Blessing I’ll Never forge.”
