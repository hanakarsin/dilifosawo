    'Too Hot to Handle's Flavia Says She Is "Not Going To Date a Singer Ever Again" After Joao Breakup

    Flavia Laos first appeared in Season 4 of 'Too Hot to Handle.'

    By Chrissy Bobic

    Updated Aug. 2 2024, 12:02 p.m. ET

    When Flavia Laos first appeared in the Netflix dating show Too Hot to Handle in Season 4, it was as a surprise addition to the group. She arrived ready to shake things up and she did for a little while. But when she left the season empty-handed (as in, with no partner), she went on with her life. However, how she's back as another bombshell, grenade, or whatever else you want to call her in Season 6.

    Because Flavia gave dating a try outside of the show as well as on the series multiple times, it makes you wonder if Flavia is dating anyone now, after Too Hot to Handle. We see her cuddle up to Joao early on in the sixth season, but filming ended well before the season dropped on Netflix. And now, she's in a very different place IRL.

    Who is Flavia Laos dating after 'Too Hot to Handle'?

    If you look at Flavia's Instagram, you might see her cozying up to different Latino singers, but that's mostly because she is no stranger to collaborating with them on various projects. And right now, it looks like she is 100 percent single, despite looking for love on Too Hot to Handle. In June 2024, Flavia shared as much with ABC in Spain.

    "I am single and dedicating myself more to myself and my work, which really takes up my entire day," she told the outlet, translated from Spanish. "So now, sharing my time with someone is super complicated. But I still don't close the doors, right? You never know."

    Are Flavia and Joao together now? He wrote a breakup song for her.

    Because Flavia so willingly told ABC that she was single in June 2024, after filming for the show had presumably ended, it's safe to say that she and Joao aren't dating now. They also had that pretty uncomfy breakup during the last couple of episodes which resulted in Joao writing and performing a breakup song for Flavia.

    Joao's break-up song for Flavia even forces her to declare, "I'm not going to date a singer ever again." For what it's worth, Joao admits that the song was a bad idea. But Flavia is still better off moving on from her short-lived Too Hot to Handle romance.

    Flavia's job outside of 'Too Hot to Handle' keeps her busy.

    Flavia is an influencer, as are many of Too Hot to Handle's stars of the past and present. However, she also has experience acting and modeling, and she's even a singer. It's no wonder that she is still single, since her triple threat of a career means she is no stranger to traveling and working nearly constantly. For now, it appears, she prefers it that way.
