    I Have Been Dealing And Managing But It Keeps Getting Worse

    Nollywood actress, Kehinde Bankole has hinted at a personal struggle via her Instagram page. The script interpreter disclosed on Thursday that she has been battling with unnamed personal issues that is getting worse for years.

    04 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Nollywood actress, Kehinde Bankole has hinted at a personal struggle via her Instagram page.

    The script interpreter disclosed on Thursday that she has been battling with unnamed personal issues that is getting worse for years.

    She said that she wants to use her platform to share her struggle as she never knows who she’d be encouraging with it.

    Promising to share her the struggle in a few days, Kehinde wrote: “There is something personal that I want to make an announcement about in a few days. It’s really important to me that I have been dealing with for years and have been managing but it keeps getting worse. I feel like if I loved this thing this much and I am struggling with it, why don’t I come and share my journey with you my friends, my fans, my lovers because I don’t think we should come online to share glamorous things. I never know who I will be encouraging, emboldening, and challenging. I will announce it in a few days.”
