    'Through The Years, My Love For You Has Remained Constant'

    Naija News reports that Frederick is one of the male Nollywood stars whom many people have been looking forward to his wedding. However, the actor who seemed to be hiding his relationship sparked mixed reactions with his birthday message to Peggy.

    29 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Nollywood actor, Frederick Leonard, has unveiled his colleague, Peggy Ovire, as the special woman in his life in a recent social media post.

    Naija News reports that Frederick is one of the male Nollywood stars whom many people have been looking forward to his wedding.

    However, the actor who seemed to be hiding his relationship sparked mixed reactions with his birthday message to Peggy.

    Frederick Leonard expressed love for Peggy stating that his love for her has remained constant over the years and he would celebrate her always.

    He wrote: “To Have n To Hold..

    “To Love n To Cherish..

    “In Good Times and in All Times..

    “Through The Years, My Love For You Has Remained Constant.

    “Look How Far We’ve Come Baby.

    “It’s Your Birthday Today and So the World Celebrates You..

    “But Our Love.. I Will Celebrate Always.

    “Happy Birthday @peggyovire

    “My Baby My Valentine..

    “I LOVE YOU”

    As expected many Nigerians stormed his comment section jubilating and congratulating him while some expressed surprise.
