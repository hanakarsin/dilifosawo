Bishop Oyedepo Finally Unveils Source Of His Prosperity

08 Jul 2024, 00:00

Presiding Bishop, Living Faith Church, Bishop David Oyedepo has finally broken his silence to speak on what makes him successful as one of the foremost pastors in Nigeria. Naija News reports that the Bishop noted that the real source of his prosperity cannot be unconnected to the fact that he now carts his tithe to God in trailer loads. As against the tithing preachings of popular On-Air-Personality, Daddy Freeze, who frowns against tithings, Oydepo said since 1982 till date, he had never failed in paying his tithe to God, noting that God had been faithful in lifting him up since he had been paying tithes.

BREAKING: Military jet mistakenly drops bomb on IDP camp

08 Jul 2024, 00:00

A military jet has mistakenly dropped a bomb inside the Rann IDP camp in Borno state leading to the death of about people with 120 injured. According to Premium Times, those who were injured had to be evacuated to hospitals for treatment. The Rann IDP camp is located in Kala-balge local government area and it caters for thousands of persons displaced by Boko Haram.
