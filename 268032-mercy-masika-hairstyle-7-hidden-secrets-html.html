    If you choose to transition from relaxed to natural hair and wondering which is the best protective hairstyle then why not try the twisted faux updo that Mercy Masika loves rocking. By checking out the hairstyle at Mercy Masika YouTube videos of her songs, you can see how the twisted updo makes a difference in her everyday look and works to cover up the front section of a receding hairline. This hairstyle protects the natural hair from breakage, tangling, and brittleness. And you can use bobby pins which work to decrease breakage of natural hair by enclosing the ends in a detangled protective state.It’s a simple to do hairstyle

In fact, the two braided updo is on one of the most basic hairstyles for women who want to show off their natural hair. This updo hairstyle will fit into your busy routine, and it’s a perfect fit both formal and casual occasions. Furthermore, it’s also ideal for ladies with shoulder length hair as well as those who want to cut down on time spent at a salon. The time-saving factor is important for Mercy Masika because apart from being a musician she is also a mother and wife.

    The hairstyle is an extravagant faux updo

    The hairstyle that makes Mercy Masika look picture perfect every time is an extravagant faux updo. The twist is easier to make which is advantageous for beginners. While Mercy Masika wedding remained low key, her hairstyle portrays a bold modern woman who is not afraid to take risks. The size of the twist is large and can be achieved by adding a braided hair extension of the same color that way you can end up creating a fun bun that matches your big personality. Instead of the boring protective hairstyles, Mercy opts for an extravagant faux updo that shows not only creativity but also ensures that the hairstyle doesn’t put so much strain on the hair ends.

    It’s her signature hairstyle

    Mercy Masika bio is full of various nominations and awards that her various songs have achieved and in most of those songs her signature hairstyles mostly involves creating a natural look. Unlike other gospel artists Kenya who are quick to show off their worldly possession and transition to natural hair only when its trendy, Mercy Masika prefers to let her music shine by maintaining a consistent natural hairstyle over the years.

    While speaking with Standard Media, the award-winning gospel artist was quick to point out that she prefers a low key life and doesn’t like surprises. No wonder she always sticks to black hair. Even after Mercy Masika married David, she still prefers to spend quality time at home instead of going out on romantic dates.

    Mercy Masika style is inspired by her parents

    It is clear that just by looking at various songs by Mercy Masika that her dressing style from head to toe commands respect. Although her sense of style is inspired by her church life whereby her mother is a Reverend and father a bishop; her hairstyle is manageable, wearable and has her looking fantastic.Mercy was once quoted saying "My parents taught me to dress up in a respectable and decent way,” and “You are addressed according to how you are dressed”. She went on to say that it was only after she was married that she switched up things a bit and started wearing short outfits. "But it's got to be knee-length with stockings," she said.

    Mercy Masika hair is affordable

    Every woman has experienced bad hair days that can just make you want to hide indoors and never come out. The go-to options for most women during those bad hair days is either a wig or a head wrap. But did you know that you can use Mercy Masika inspired faux hairstyle as a simple hack to manage any bad hair day. Ladies who have natural hair can quickly create a faux hair updo that is manageable and stylish. What’s even great about this hairstyle is you can make it using readily available at home materials such as a comb, elastic bands, hairspray and bobby pins.

    Her hairstyle embraces her natural hair shape

    Another hidden secret of Mercy Masika hairstyle is that you don’t have to expose your natural hair to excess heat and chemicals just to make it look good. Recently, there has been a shift towards natural hairstyles and various celebrities have not only reinforced the natural look but paved the way, and Masika has not been left behind. Mercy Masika loves her natural hair look as it complements her facial features and helps her stand out as a celebrity.

    Her signature hairstyle is done in such a way that it makes her best facial features stand out. She often completes this hairdo with a subtle makeup which includes a red or purple lipstick. For a classy look, she matches the bold hairstyle with bold accessories which include chunky earrings, necklaces, and bracelets.
