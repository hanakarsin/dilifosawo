    Became known for playing Bella Swan in The Twilight Saga, a role for which she won numerous Teen Choice and People's Choice Awards. Her other films include Zathura, Panic Room, Still Alice, and Adventureland.

    Before Fame

    She debuted in the Disney Channel film The Thirteenth Year at the age of eight. Her parents John and Jules were both in the film industry.

    Trivia

    She won a Cesar Award for Best Supporting Actress for her role in the 2014 film Clouds of Sils Maria.

    Family Life

    She had a highly publicized relationship with her Twilight co-star Robert Pattinson from 2008 to 2013. She previously dated Stella Maxwell, Alicia Cargile, St. Vincent and French pop singer Soko. She started dating Dylan Meyer in 2019, and they got engaged in November 2021.

    Associated With

    She co-starred with Charlize Theron in the 2012 film Snow White and the Huntsman.
