    Davido's Baby Mamas Yet To Share Condolence Post Over Ifeanyi Tragic Death

    23 Feb 2024, 00:00

    The baby mamas of prominent Nigerian artiste, Davido have kept mum and are yet to publicly mourn the death of the first son of the singer who passed on after drowning at his father’s Banana Island, Lagos residence.

    It is no news that Ifeanyi’s death became a public knowledge on 31st of October, sparking reactions and condolences amongst celebrities and the general public alike.

    Following the death of Davido’s son, many people around the world have taken to social media to mourn and extend prayers to the bereaved family (The Adelekes).

    But, Interestingly, the singer’s baby mamas namely, Sophia Momodu, mother of Davido’s first child, Imade. American model, Amanda, mother of Hailey, his second child and UK-based makeup artist, Larissa Yamin Lorenco from Angola, mother of his second son, Dawson are yet to make any condolence post since the tragic reports broke the internet.

    Chioma Rowland aka Chef Chi is Ifeanyi’s mum. Ifeanyi was Davido’s first son.

    Davido’s Unfollows Baby mamas On Social Media

    The deafening silence from the babymamas may not be unconnected to the events that led the singer to unceremoniously unfollow all his three baby mamas some weeks back on the photo and video-sharing app, Instagram.

    They reciprocated the gesture and refused to follow him back.

    Davido, Chioma Reignites Relationship

    The action came days after Davido and Chioma sparked reconciliation rumours after they were spotted all loved-up together.

    Chioma had broke the internet when she stepped out in style for Davido’s concert rocking a new 30BG pendant with the inscription, ‘002’, which hinted at her reconciliation with Davido.

    The music star even hinted on marrying Chioma while on a visit to famous clergyman, Pastor Tobi Adegboyega.

    The two, until the death of Ifeanyi, were seen everywhere together rocking the same outfit.

    However, despite the silence from the babymamas, top personalities have taken to their social media to express sadness over the tragedy that befell the Adeleke’s.
