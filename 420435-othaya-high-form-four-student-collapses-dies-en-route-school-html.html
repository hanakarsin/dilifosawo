    A source intimated to TUKO.co.ke that after collapsing, the student was rushed to Kenyatta National Hospital-Othaya Annex by a good samaritan where he was pronounced dead.

    His body was then transferred to Consolata Nursing Home for preservation.

    Mutinda was described as a humble, hardworking and visionary student.

    Mutinda came from poor family

    Mutinda's death was a big blow to his family, which is reportedly living in dire poverty.

    TUKO.co.ke learned that untill his death, his education was being sponsored by Equity Bank.

    Further, Mutinda's mother is bedridden after developing health complications about three years ago.

    The source added that Mutinda's siblings were too dependent on well-wishers for school fees and their basic needs.
