    Season 17 of America's Got Talent has already introduced viewers to the work of rising stars like Amanda Mammana, Lily Meola, and Avery Dixon. Twenty-one-year-old Pittsburgh native Aubrey Burchell took to the stage in the episode that aired on Tuesday, Aug. 2, 2022, and she immediately impressed the judges and long-time fans of the show.
