    Former WBA and IBF heavyweight champion Tyson Fury has blasted British-Nigerian boxer Anthony Joshua stating he is not a 'true' Nigerian

    The Undisputed British boxer in his recent post on Instagram said he is more of a Nigerian than Joshua as he challenged the current WBA and IBF heavyweight champion to a fight.

    The 27-year-old told Skysport: “He didn’t throw a punch. He didn’t come to fight.”

    “He just walked around the ring for like 12 rounds, it’s boring. It’s not winning, it’s how you win."
