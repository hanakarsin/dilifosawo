    Has Alfred Mutua ever met Judy Jepchirchir?

    Appearing before the Committee on Appointments on Sunday, August 4, Mutua denied personally knowing Jepchirchir.

    Mutua disclosed that he met her once at the Kenyatta International Conference Centre (KICC) and introduced her to President William Ruto.

    "I don't know Jepchirchir personally. I only met her once at KICC when I served as Foreign Affairs CS. Her stand was close to that of the Labour ministry, and someone told me what she was doing to create jobs. That is how I introduced her to President Ruto then. But I don't know her and I have never met her again. Later on, I saw in the media what was happening," Mutua said.

    Jepchirchir came to the limelight in 2023 when youthful job seekers from Eldoret took to the streets to demand a refund of the money they had paid to her agency.

    The First Choice Recruitment agency has been in the limelight for allegedly scamming Eldoret residents millions of shillings.

    As reported earlier, the First Choice Recruitment and Consulting Agency director admitted failing to take people to Qatar for jobs.

    This followed complaints by 10,000 youths from Uasin Gishu and Nandi counties that the agency defrauded them over KSh 400 million in the overseas job programme

    Jepchirchir shared how the money received was spent, saying most people changed their contract while others are due for a refund.

    The businesswoman said the applicants on a short-term contract paid a KSh 10,000 medical fee (non-refundable) and a KSh 30,000 service charge.
