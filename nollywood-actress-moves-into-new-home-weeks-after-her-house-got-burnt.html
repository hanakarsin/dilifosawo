    Nollywood Actress Moves Into New Home Weeks After Her House Got Burnt

    The actress stated that since the unfortunate incident that led to her home being burnt down she has been residing in a hotel. Ekanem took to her Instagram page to announce the good news while also thanking her fans and colleagues for their support during the trying times.

    08 May 2024, 00:00

    Nollywood actress, Didi Ekanem has moved into a new home few weeks after her house got burnt in a fire accident.

    The actress stated that since the unfortunate incident that led to her home being burnt down she has been residing in a hotel.

    Ekanem took to her Instagram page to announce the good news while also thanking her fans and colleagues for their support during the trying times.

    She wrote: “Moving into a new house is not easy 0 Today is our last day in the hotel and all I can say is thank You Lord. In love with my new house and it can only be God Almighty.”

    In other news, controversial journalist, Kemi Olunloyo has debunked claims that her Twitter was suspended over a post she made.

    Naija News reports that Kemi Olunloyo had claimed late Sylvester Oromoni willing joined a cult in his school, Dowen College, adding that he consented to be beaten and given engine oil.

    Angered by the revelation, veteran Nollywood actress, Ada Ameh threatened to beat up the journalist.

    Reacting to Ada’s outburst on Twitter, Kemi revealed she had reported the actress to the police and the Department of State Service for threatening to beat her up.
