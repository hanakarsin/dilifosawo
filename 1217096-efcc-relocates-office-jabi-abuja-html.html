    The anti-graft agency, the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC), said it has relocated its headquarter from their former place and now situated it at Jabi, Abuja.

    The commission made this known to members of the public in a post available on its Facebook page.

    EFCC, Legit.ng gathers, also added that its operational office can be found at 301/3012 Research and Institution Cadastral District, Jabi, Abuja
