    14 Jun 2024, 00:00

    The All Progressives Congress (APC), on Monday, inaugurated its Women Presidential Campaign Council for the 2023 general election.

    Naija News reports that the inauguration was done by President Muhmmadu Buhari, who was represented by his Chief of Staff, Professor Ibrahim Gambari.

    The event which was held at the Banquet Hall of the Presidential Villa, Abuja, had the National Chairman of the party, Senator Abdullahi Adamu, and members of the National Working Committee (NWC) in attendance.

    The APC presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu, and his running mate, Kashim Shettima, were also present at the event.

    Also at the inauguration were the First Lady, Aisha Buhari and wives of the APC presidential candidate and vice presidential candidate, Remi Tinubu and Nana Shettima respectively.

    Others are the Chairman of the Presidential Campaign Council (PCC), Governor Simon Lalong; Governor Hope Uzodinma of Imo; Abdullahi Ganduje of Kano State; Women Leader of the APC, Beta Edu; other leaders of the party; National Assembly members and others.
